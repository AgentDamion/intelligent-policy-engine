# AICOMPLYR & NY Synthetic Performer Disclosure Law

## What the Law Requires

- **Conspicuous disclosure** when ads contain AI-generated human likenesses
- Applies to advertisements distributed in New York
- Effective: June 2026
- Penalties: $1,000-$5,000 per violation
